Course Launches

The first release from the Human Body Academy is a revision guide Anatomy & Physiology - Ace Your MCQ Exams, The Ultimate Revision Guide. It is a comprehensive bank of multiple choice questions, essential diagrams and definitions for students with upcoming exams. This is not a taught course as others are but rather a self paced resource including over 1000 multiple choice questions divided by the systems of the body . These questions are auto-corrected and may be practiced as often as the student wishes. My reasoning for launching this first is that, in over 27 years of preparing students for exams in Anatomy & Physiology, practice multiple choice questions were the most requested resource.

The Digestive System - Everything You Need To Know About The Digestive System..... for students of Esthetics, Allied Health & Complementary Health is the first of the courses to be launched by HBA. This course has video lessons comprehensively teaching about the digestive system ....the ABC of the digestive system without waffle. Other study resources on the digestive system are provided to help you in your studies and give you confidence in your knowledge of the anatomy of the digestive system and how we digest food and absorb nutrients.
